How It Works

Our Simple 4-Step Process

From first contact to final permit-ready deliverable — transparent, fast, and stress-free.

01

Share Project Details

Send us your project info — site address, roof photos, equipment specs, or existing drawings. Email, WhatsApp, or our inquiry form all work.

02

Design & Drafting

Our engineers and CAD drafters create your drawings to AHJ standards. We research local code requirements and format everything for your jurisdiction.

03

Quality Check

Every deliverable goes through a rigorous internal review — dimensions, code compliance, equipment data, and title block accuracy all verified before sending.

04

Final Delivery

You receive PDF and DWG files, ready to submit. We support you through any AHJ comment cycles with fast, included revisions until your permit is approved.
